# Limen

Navigate professional transitions with clarity.

Limen is an AI-native career transition platform designed to help people navigate uncertainty, identify meaningful opportunities, and move toward their next professional chapter with confidence.

Instead of optimizing for endless applications, Limen focuses on thoughtful career movement — helping users understand what to learn, where to apply, how to position themselves, and which opportunities are truly worth pursuing.

The platform combines career intelligence, opportunity evaluation, strategic guidance, and human-centered AI workflows to support people through periods of professional change.

---

## Product

### Limen Guide

An intelligent career transition system that helps users:

- discover relevant opportunities
- identify skill gaps
- plan learning paths
- prepare applications
- evaluate career moves
- navigate uncertainty with structure and clarity

---

## Internal Runtime

### Bounded Decision Runtime (BDR)

A policy-driven decision system responsible for:

- opportunity scoring
- fit evaluation
- uncertainty estimation
- action routing
- escalation logic
- human-in-the-loop decision workflows
